About Us

WELCOME TO BKS RESTURANT

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, the 5-star Hotel De L' BKS RESTURANT is less than a 5-minute stroll from Durbar Marg Avenue. This fine hotel offers a fitness centre and a garden as well as guest rooms with views of the garden.

The rooms at the De L' BKSresturant feature a sofa set and a work desk, along with soundproof windows for guests' convenience. Also, there is a mini fridge bar as well as a safe and an electronic key card provided. The restaurant at the property serves a buffet breakfast every morning. A lounge bar is available on site. Set near this Kathmandu hotel, the restaurant Korea Pyongyang Arirang Restaurant offers an Asian menu.
